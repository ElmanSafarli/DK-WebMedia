our development process
When it comes to web and app development, DK WebMedia handles everything from A to Z and designed a process to ensure that amazing websites and apps are developed.
UI/UX Design and Copywriting
The first stage is to Design the Website or App using Figma. Our design are all modern and marketing oriented to make the client buy. In the first stage we also write the content which is designed to have amazing SEO results.
Development and Programming
The second stage is where the magic happens, we write the code to build the website or the app. Our code is always clean, minimized and optimized. The websites always work on all devices and our apps work on all phones and ipads IOS and android.
Hosting & Domain name
